01 — Problem Statement

Ethan Hetzler is a University of Iowa Tippie College of Business student pursuing a B.B.A. in Economics and a B.B.A. in Business Analytics and Information Systems, with an anticipated graduation date of May 2026.

Ethan has accepted a position with Equitable Advisors in Kansas City as a financial advisor. The purpose of this landing page is to introduce him in a way that feels formal but approachable, helping visitors quickly understand his education, leadership, and relevant experience.

The professional problem this page solves is that a resume alone does not create enough trust or personal connection for prospective clients. This landing page should act as a curated professional introduction that helps visitors see Ethan as trustworthy, knowledgeable, and worth contacting for help with their financial goals.

02 — Target Audience

The primary audience is young professionals in the Kansas City area who are beginning to think seriously about long-term financial planning, investing, and building a strong foundation for their future.

These visitors are likely looking for:

Ethan’s background
Ethan’s values
Signs of credibility and professionalism
A simple, direct way to contact him

This page is not primarily designed for broad job-search visibility. Its first purpose is to support Ethan’s early professional presence as a new financial professional serving a local audience.

03 — Goals & Success Metrics
Goal	How We Measure It
Generate early client interest	5 new business leads within 30–60 days after launch
Create direct inbound contact opportunities	Count leads as any email, text, or phone call received from the page or because of the page
Build trust with first-time visitors	Visitors can quickly identify Ethan’s background, values, and relevant experience from one page
Make outreach easy	Contact details and professional links are visible and easy to use
