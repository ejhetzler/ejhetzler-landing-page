About Me

I am a student at the University of Iowa Tippie College of Business, completing a B.B.A. in Economics and a B.B.A. in Business Analytics and Information Systems, with an anticipated graduation date of May 2026.

After graduation, I will be joining Equitable Advisors in Kansas City as a financial advisor. My goal is to work closely with young professionals who are beginning to think seriously about long-term financial planning, investing, and building a strong financial foundation for their future.

I believe trust is the foundation of any great client relationship. That is why I focus on clear communication, genuine connection, and delivering advice that is grounded in both data and personal understanding.
